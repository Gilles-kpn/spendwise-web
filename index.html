Confidentiality Rules for Spendwise

Extended Confidentiality Rules for Spendwise Application

Welcome to Spendwise, a financial management application designed to empower users in managing their expenses efficiently. Ensuring the privacy and security of your data is our top priority.

1. Shared Data

Spendwise adheres to strict data sharing practices. As a user, you can trust that no data is shared with third parties. The developer, in accordance with our commitment to user privacy, explicitly states that the application does not share any user data with external companies or organizations. Your information remains confidential and secure within the Spendwise ecosystem.

2. Collected Data

In the course of providing our services, Spendwise may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and user ID. These details are collected to personalize your experience and facilitate essential functionalities within the app.
3. Data Deletion

Spendwise recognizes the importance of user control over their data. While users have the option to delete their account and associated data, it's important to note that the developer has not currently provided a direct mechanism within the app for users to request data deletion. The deletion process can be initiated by submitting a request through the provided link: https://gilles-kpn.github.io/spenwise/.

4. Security Practices

We prioritize the security of your data through robust encryption measures:

  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted during transfer, ensuring that your information is protected against unauthorized access.
  • Secure Connection: Your data is transmitted through a secure connection, further enhancing the confidentiality of your financial information.
5. Inability to Delete Data

While users have the option to delete their accounts, the developer has not integrated a direct feature within the app to request the deletion of specific data. Users are encouraged to refer to the privacy policy for comprehensive insights into data handling practices.

6. Additional Information

For a more in-depth understanding of how Spendwise manages user data, we encourage users to review the developer's privacy policy. This document provides detailed insights into the collection, use, and protection of your data, reinforcing our commitment to transparency and user trust.
